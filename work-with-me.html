About your approach

Colour is the hidden language of the soul, and only confidence that comes from your soul is ever lasting

When you first hear the word colour, you think of light. But close your eyes for a second and what do you see? A myriad of brilliant colours dancing in front of you, like a kaleidoscope. Even in a dark room, you’d still see a world of colours, many of which would be impossible to reproduce in hysical reality. Colour is far, far more than a mere visual delight. In fact, it has been telling us something important about ourselves throughout aeons of evolution. But in an underlying manner. Because colour is all about feelings, and feelings are subtle. As a sensitive woman, you are far more capable of grasping this subtleness. You dream in colour, you visualise in colour, you imagine in colour. So knowing how colour affects you will make you think more carefully about every decision you make in your daily life.

Colour Analysis

Colour analysis considers your physical colouring. The skin acts as a filter and the tone lies just under the surface. Knowing the skin tone will enable you to wear your best colours, more exactly the shade and intensity of the colour that suits you most. Everyone can wear almost any colour. The correct colour analysis will give you your very individual colour spectrum. You will know which colours to wear, which ones to avoid and what Season you are.

Colour Psychology

Colour psychology allows for the understanding of personality types and their response to colours. We are all unique individuals, yet there are recognisable patterns of typical behaviour and characteristics which are clearly defined. Knowing and understanding your behaviour and traits permits ultimately your understanding of self and of others.

While the two systems described above are essential for our work together, I want you to know that I work with the individual, not the rules of colour analysis.

Because physical colour analysis is designed to fit segments of people.
