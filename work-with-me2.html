Who I am.

JOURNEY

I was born loving to dress...

My parents instilled in me a natural appreciation for elegance, the importance of presentation and manners.

Originally from a science background, my soul yearned to experience diversity and raw adventure. Fast forward across many years in the delicious pot of a very vibrant Rome, a crisp and formal Frankfurt, the heady zing and flavour of Budapest and one of my favourite cities in the world – Tel Aviv, a sexy City where one feels truly alive. Eventually I settled here in Australia, where I feel an innate sense of freedom. Australia allowed me to find my own style. It didn’t require so much, it allowed me to become who I wanted to be.

With the backdrop and influences of my experiences around the world combined with a lifelong fascination for psychology, I embarked on the study on my life’s great work – Colour, how it can heal, transform our lives and better understand ourselves and others. My journey brought me to my mission of what I do today, to awaken others through my work and my responsibility to offer it, to you and women around the world.
